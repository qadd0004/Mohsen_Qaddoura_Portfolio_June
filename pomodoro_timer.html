The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. The technique uses a timer to break down study / work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks (Wikipedia). Thus this timer has been capped at 60 minutes so as not to nullify the Pomodoro Technique sought after benefits of better grades and work quality.
